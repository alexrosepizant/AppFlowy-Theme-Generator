Export Theme
Download a .zip ready to import into AppFlowy
1.Download the .zip
2.Unzip → you get a folder named Name.flowy_plugin
3.Open AppFlowy → Settings → Appearance → click “Upload theme”
4.Select the .flowy_plugin folder
5.Choose your theme from the list — done!
